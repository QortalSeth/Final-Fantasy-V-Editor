_________

ITEM DATA
_________

* D1/0000-05FF: Weapons                       [Item] (00-7F)
* D1/0600-0A7F: Armor                         [Item] (80-DF)
* D1/0A80-0B7F: Usables                       [Item] (E0-FF)
- D1/2480-257F: [Equipment type] definitions  Equipment type value
* D1/2580-26BF: Element defense definitions   [Element defense]
* D1/26C0-287F: Status properties definitions [Status defense]
